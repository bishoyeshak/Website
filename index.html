How it works

Three steps to finding
your community.

1

Apply & verify

Sign up with your school email. We verify GPA and school to keep the community focused and motivated.

2

Pick your interests

Select one or more fields — STEM, business, medicine, arts, law. You're placed in those group chats immediately.

3

Connect & discover

Ask questions, share opportunities, or DM someone who's done what you want to do.

Membership requirements

No resume needed — just three clear criteria to keep the community quality-first.

  • 4.2+ weighted GPA Ensures a motivated, college-bound community
  • 4+ extracurriculars Programs, clubs, or activities outside of class
