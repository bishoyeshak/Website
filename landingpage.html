Our mission

Building connections that actually matter.

LinkedTeen helps high school students discover opportunities, collaborate with motivated peers, and access mentorship — all in a safe, supportive environment designed with students in mind.

Find your people

Join interest-based communities — STEM, business, medicine, arts, law — and meet students who share your exact ambitions at schools across your district.

Discover opportunities

Surface programs, competitions, internships, and extracurriculars from peers who've actually done them — real insight, no guesswork.

Mentorship that fits

Get guidance from older students who've navigated the exact path you're on — college apps, summer programs, club leadership, and beyond.

How it works

Three steps to your community.

1

Apply & verify

Sign up with your school email. We confirm GPA and enrollment to keep the community motivated and high-quality.

2

Pick your interests

Choose one or more fields. You're placed directly into live group chats with students who share your focus.

3

Connect & grow

Ask questions, share opportunities, or DM someone who's already done what you want to do next.
